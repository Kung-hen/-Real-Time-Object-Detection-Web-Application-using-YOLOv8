Object Detection using YOLOv8

Video Frame Rate (FPS)

The FPS (Frames Per Second) represents the frequency at which consecutive frames are displayed in the video. When utilizing GPU acceleration, the processing speed is optimized, resulting in a higher FPS. Conversely, processing on CPU tends to yield a lower FPS due to its comparatively slower computational capabilities.

Resolution of Current Frame

The resolution, or dimensions, of the current frame are determined, providing insight into the clarity and detail of the image being processed. This information is crucial for understanding the quality of the visual data being analyzed.

Detections in Current Frame

For each frame, the algorithm identifies and quantifies the total number of object detections present. This analysis provides valuable data regarding the frequency and distribution of objects within the video stream, facilitating further analysis and decision-making processes.
